## Plain Text Version

```
Subject: Meet TAAI: Your New 24/7 Tech Support Assistant

Hi Team,

We're excited to introduce Tech Assist AI (TAAI) — your intelligent tech support assistant that's available 24/7 to help resolve common IT issues instantly.

WHY TAAI?

We know tech issues can slow you down. TAAI is here to help you get back to work faster:

• Instant Answers – No more wait times. Get help in seconds, not minutes.
• 24/7 Availability – Need help at 2 AM? TAAI never sleeps.
• Smart & Accurate – Powered by AI, trained on our internal knowledge base.
• Human Handoff – Complex issue? TAAI seamlessly escalates to Tech Assist.

WHAT CAN TAAI HELP WITH?

• Password resets and account lockouts
• VPN connection issues
• Email configuration
• Software installation and access
• Hardware requests
• ...and much more!

HOW TO GET STARTED

1. Visit taai.macquarie.com or find TAAI on the Tech Assist SharePoint
2. Type your question in plain English
3. Get instant, step-by-step guidance
4. Escalate to a human agent if needed

Pro Tip: You can also access TAAI directly in Microsoft Teams! Just search for "Tech Assist AI" in the Apps section.

YOUR TECH ASSIST TEAM IS STILL HERE

TAAI complements our human Tech Assist team — it doesn't replace them. For complex issues, TAAI will seamlessly connect you with an agent. This frees up Tech Assist to focus on more challenging problems, improving support for everyone.

WE WANT YOUR FEEDBACK

TAAI is continuously learning and improving. After each interaction, you'll be asked to rate your experience. Your feedback helps us make TAAI even better.

Try TAAI now: https://taai.macquarie.com

Questions about TAAI? Visit our FAQ page at sharepoint.macquarie.com/taai/faq or contact us at tech-assist-ai@macquarie.com

The Digital Innovation Team
Technology Services
Macquarie Group
```

## Send Strategy

### Phase 1: Executive Announcement (Week 1)
- **Audience**: All employees
- **Sender**: CTO or Technology Services Director
- **Timing**: Monday, 9:00 AM AEST
- **Follow-up**: Yammer post, Teams announcement

### Phase 2: Reminder (Week 2)
- **Subject**: "Have You Tried TAAI Yet? 🤖"
- **Highlight**: Early success stories, quick stats
- **CTA**: Encourage first-time users

### Phase 3: Feature Spotlight (Week 3-4)
- **Subject**: "TAAI Pro Tips: Get More Done Faster"
- **Content**: Advanced features, tips & tricks
- **Include**: Video tutorials, common use cases

---

## Success Metrics to Include in Follow-Up Emails

After 1 Month:
- "TAAI has resolved X,XXX queries in the first month"
- "Average resolution time: X seconds"
- "XX% of issues resolved without Tech Assist escalation"
- "Users saved XX,XXX hours collectively"

Include testimonials:
> "TAAI helped me reset my VPN in 30 seconds. Would have taken 15 minutes on the phone!" — Sarah M., Sydney

## A/B Testing Recommendations

Test these subject line variants:
- A: "🤖 Meet TAAI: Your New 24/7 Tech Support Assistant" (Emoji + Feature)
- B: "No More Wait Times: Introducing Tech Assist AI" (Problem + Solution)
- C: "Tech Help in Seconds: Try Our New AI Assistant" (Speed Benefit)

Track:
- Open rates

## Email Suppression Rules

Do NOT send to:
- Users on leave (auto-exclude)
- External contractors (different support process)
- Suspended accounts
